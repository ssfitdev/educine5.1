Welcome to the Educine Pitch Deck

The Entrepreneurship Pitch Deck at Educine provides a platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to present their ideas, receive expert feedback, and explore opportunities for growth and collaboration.

Please Read the Following Instructions Carefully:

Eligibility: Open to students, aspiring entrepreneurs, and early-stage innovators with original ideas.
Individual / Team Participation: Participants can register individually or as a team (maximum 5 members).
Pitch Format: Each participant/team will present their idea using a pitch deck (PPT/PDF). Duration: 5-7 minutes presentation + Q&A.
Originality: All ideas must be original. Plagiarism or copied concepts will lead to disqualification.
Selection Process: Entries may be shortlisted based on the information provided in this form.
Submission Requirement: Final pitch deck must be submitted before the deadline (details will be shared).
Consent: By registering, you agree to allow organizers to use your pitch details for evaluation and event-related purposes.
